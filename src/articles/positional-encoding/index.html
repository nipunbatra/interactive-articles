Positional Encodings — Sinusoid, RoPE, ALiBi

Transformer attention is permutation-invariant. "dog bites man" and "man bites dog" would have identical logits. Position must come from somewhere else. Three families of solutions — pick whichever extrapolates to long context best.

~15 min Transformers · Long Context
Step 1

Sinusoidal (Vaswani 2017)

$\text{PE}_{(\text{pos}, 2i)} = \sin(\text{pos} / 10000^{2i/d})$, $\text{PE}_{(\text{pos}, 2i+1)} = \cos(\ldots)$.

Added to the token embedding. Different frequencies in different dimensions; relative position is recoverable by linear combinations.

Step 2

RoPE (Su 2021, Llama default)

Apply a 2-D rotation to query and key, with angle proportional to position. The dot-product $q \cdot k$ then depends only on the relative position.

Step 3

ALiBi (Press 2022)

Skip embeddings entirely. Add a linear bias $-m \cdot |i - j|$ to attention scores. Brutally simple, extrapolates well past training length.
